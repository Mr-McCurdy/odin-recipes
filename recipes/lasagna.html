Simple Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

For Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 ounces)
  • 2 cans tomato paste (6 ounces each)

For Cheese Mixture:

  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles until al dente. Rinse with cold water, and drain.
  3. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook ground beef with garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine grated Parmesan, basil, oregano, beaten eggs, ricotta, and mozzarella. Mix well.
  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread 1 cup of meat sauce on the bottom of dish. Place 6 lasagna noodles on top of sauce. Spread one half of cheese mixture over noodles. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce, and sprinkle extra parmesan on top.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and brown. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
