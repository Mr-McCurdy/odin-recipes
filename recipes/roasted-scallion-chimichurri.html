Sticky Turkey Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 6 scallions
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 bunch mint
  • 1 bunch flatleaf parsley
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup seasoned rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon agave nectar
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 garlic cloves

Instructions:

  1. broil scallions until browned (some black is OK, it adds a bit of a smokey flavor)
  2. wash and chop herbs
  3. smash garlic
  4. food processor combine
  5. store and enjoy!
