Citrus Spiced Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • handful of shrimp deshelled, deveigned
  • 2 heaping pinches madras curry powder
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions:

Prepare Pepper Paste

  1. after drying shrimp, mix with spices and salt (bowl recommended)
  2. heat skillet (prefer wok personally) with garlic oil
  3. Add salt to taste.
  4. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook ground beef with garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, then simmer for 15 minutes.

Prepare Beef

  1. Pat the meat dry.
  2. Slice beef into chucks (approx 3 inches).
  3. Combine beef and pepper paste, let marinate.

Cook Beef

  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees F.
  2. Oil roasting pan (with elevated sides).
  3. Combine sliced onion and marinated beef into pan.
  4. Roast until brown, flip.
  5. Add stock (approx 1 cup)
  6. Roast at 225 F until beef pulls apart easily, flipping on occasion.
