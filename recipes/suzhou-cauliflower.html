Sticky Turkey Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 heaping pinches ground cumin
  • 2 pinches cayenne
  • 1 heaping pinch salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine roasted garlic, roasted red peppers (without liquid), and paprika.
  2. Grind aforesaid mixture into a paste (food processor, mortar/pestle).
  3. Add salt to taste.
  4. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook ground beef with garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, then simmer for 15 minutes.
