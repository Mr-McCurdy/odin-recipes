Suzhou Cauliflower

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 heaping pinches ground cumin
  • 2 pinches cayenne
  • 1 heaping pinch salt
  • olive oil (enough to coat cauliflower)

Instructions:

  1. preheat oven 350 F
  2. combine all ingredients in bowl, mix well
  3. roast in roasting pan at 350 F until brown
  4. lower heat to 250 F and continue cooking until tender
