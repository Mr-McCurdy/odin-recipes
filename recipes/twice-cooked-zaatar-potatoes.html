Simple Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

For Red Pepper Beef:

  • 1 lb baby yukon gold potatoes
  • quarter cup zaatar
  • olive oil
  • salt

Instructions:

First cook

  1. roast potatoes at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
  2. gently Smash with back of spoon while hot to flatten
  3. let cool

Second cook

  1. coat potates in olive oil (it's ok to be generous)
  2. coat evenly (hands+bowl recommended) with the zaatar
  3. roast at 250 F, occasionaly basting with olive oil until crispy and delicious
